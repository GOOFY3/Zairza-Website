Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you get started

Why Zairza?

For people persuing careers in computers and/or robotics, writing code, developing softwares, and creating exceptional things. That's what we do here. We do things and we help people who are really interested into them.

What are the pre-requisites to join here?

As a learner, there are no requirements to join here, only that you have to pay the registeration fees. We will provide you with all the necessary things you need to do.

What is the scope of Zairza?

As mentioned, Zairza is managed by the CET's strong Alumni base. So, it will be really helpful for anybody who wants to know about any specific thing or is stuck somewhere in between.

Does Robotics deal with AI that has emotions?

AI's most certainly will have emotions. Emotions are defined as low-level/ instinctive drives and anticipation of rewards. Most recently, Facebook created an AI which was trained a specific language but after a while, it developed its own language and it was finally shut down.

Does learning different Computer languages help improve your coding skills?

No. Generally, all the languages have the same logic or argument. Leaning any language to the full can make you a good coder. However, languages like Python use a large database of libraries that come in really handy when you have to import certain codes in your program.
